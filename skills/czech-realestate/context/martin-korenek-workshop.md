## Investment Philosophy

- **Net worth is the only metric that matters.** Track it, optimize for it.
- Use as little of your own cash as possible - maximize leverage.
- Buy assets with a long-term perspective. Sell only if the asset underperforms AND a better opportunity exists.
- Compound interest (slozene uroceni) is the engine. Time in market beats timing the market.
- "Perfection kills." An 80% good deal acted on fast beats a 100% deal found too late.

## The Law of Convergence (Zakon konvergence)

Regional markets catch up - secondary cities grow faster than Prague over time. This is the core thesis. Current data supports it.

**How to identify undervalued locations:**
1. Use Sreality price maps: https://www.sreality.cz/cenova-mapa
2. Track price growth over time: https://www.sreality.cz/ceny-nemovitosti
3. Cross-reference with Valuo (https://www.valuo.cz/) and REAS (https://reas.cz/)
4. Compare rental listings vs. properties for sale vs. population size to judge demand/supply

## Acquisition Strategy

- **Speed over negotiation.** Minimal bargaining, quick deals.
- Work with brokers who know you're a ready buyer - get offers before public listings.
- Appraisers ("znalci") determine fair value for financing - maintain relationships with them.
- Prioritize financing and acquisition; delegate renovation, tenancy, operations.
- Investor's Triangle: balance yield, growth potential, and risk.

**Red flags:** shrinking population, cooperative ownership (druzstevni), no infrastructure, poor energy rating.
**Green flags:** personal ownership (osobni), good transport, growing population, below-average price/m2.

## Financing Models

### Model 1: FO vs. PO Structure
- **FO** = Fyzicka osoba (Individual). **PO** = Pravnicka osoba (Legal Entity / SRO).
- Always purchase as FO first - best financing conditions.

### Model 2: Backward Refinancing (Zpetne refinancovani)
1. Build base portfolio (e.g., 5 flats)
2. After 1+ year appreciation, get appraiser to update valuations
3. Bank A: consolidate existing loans to cover 3 flats (higher combined value)
4. 2 flats become unencumbered (bez zastavy)
5. Bank B: new mortgage on free flats - unlocks equity as tax-free cash
- **Good banks:** Hypotecni banka (flexible). **Avoid:** UniCredit (rigid).

### Model 3: Decoy Strategy
- When backward refinancing is blocked (needs 2-3 years): sell flat, repurchase through PO.

### Model 4: Gratuitous Transfer (Bezuplatny prevod)
- After time test (casovy test): transfer between FO and PO tax-free.
- Used for long-term portfolio optimization and inheritance planning.

### Model 5: SRO Company Loan
- Banks: Fio, Moneta, Raiffeisen (up to CZK 11.5M)
- Buy 1-2 flats under FO -> use as collateral -> PO borrows up to CZK 30M -> PO lends to FO -> FO buys more

### Model 8: When to Sell
- Only when asset underperforms relative to portfolio AND better opportunity exists.

### Model 9: Bianco Termination Clause
- Allows early exit or refinancing flexibility without waiting for fixed terms to expire.

## Key Principles (Summary)

1. Always leverage FO status for acquisitions
2. Maintain appraiser relationships to influence valuations positively
3. Unlock equity without selling via backward refinancing or FO-PO structures
4. Think long-term: prepare for time-test rules when planning transfers
5. Bank relationships matter: know which banks are investor-friendly
6. DSTI (Debt Service to Income ratio) and Paragraph 9 rules govern lending limits
